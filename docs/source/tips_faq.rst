Tips and Frequently Asked Questions 
=====

.. important::
    This is a tool being developed by the ACACIA project. Please email feedback to joshua.talib@ecmwf.int.

Tips
-----------

1. Due to license restrictions, public ECMWF Confluence accounts cannot view certain reference pages (for example, the `available forecasting systems <https://confluence.ecmwf.int/display/~ecm0847/acacia_s2s_toolkit+available+forecasting+systems>`_ page). To access such publicly viewable pages, please log out of your ECMWF account and view them anonymously (i.e., while not logged in). 
2. If you are finding that your download is stalled, you can check the status of your webAPI downloads on the following `page <https://apps.ecmwf.int/webmars/joblist/>`_.
